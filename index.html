Amazon Customer Data Analysis

Utilized Python, Numpy, Pandas, and Matplotlib to analyze Amazon reviews from a SQLite database. Cleaned and transformed the data, identified top users' product preferences, and analyzed highly reviewed products. Compared behaviors of frequent and non-frequent reviewers, finding frequent reviewers to be more verbose. Conducted sentiment analysis to identify key positive and negative review keywords.
